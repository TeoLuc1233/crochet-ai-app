# Crochet AI User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click "Sign Up" in the header
2. Enter your username, email, and password
3. Click "Register"
4. You'll be automatically logged in

### Generating a Pattern

1. Navigate to the "Generate" page
2. Upload a photo of a crochet item (drag and drop or click to select)
3. Click "Generate Pattern"
4. Wait for the AI to analyze and generate your pattern
5. Review and edit the generated pattern if needed
6. Save to your projects or export

### Browsing Patterns

1. Go to the "Patterns" page
2. Use filters to find patterns by:
   - Difficulty level
   - Category
   - Materials
   - Premium/Free
3. Click on a pattern to view full details

### Managing Projects

1. Go to "Projects" page
2. Click "New Project" to create a project
3. Link it to a pattern (optional)
4. Track your progress with the progress bar
5. Add notes as you work

### Subscriptions

- **Free**: Browse free patterns, create up to 3 projects
- **Premium**: All free features + premium patterns + 10 AI generations/month
- **Pro**: Unlimited AI generations + advanced features

To subscribe:
1. Go to "Pricing" page
2. Select your plan
3. Complete checkout via Stripe
4. Your account will be upgraded automatically

## Tips

- Use high-quality photos for better pattern generation
- Edit generated patterns to match your preferences
- Save your favorite patterns to projects
- Track your progress to stay motivated
